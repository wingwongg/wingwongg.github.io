PRIVACY POLICY OF RECLAIMR

Last updated: May 8, 2025

Reclaimr is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines what data we collect and how we handle it.

THE DATA WE COLLECT

Reclaimr may collect the following types of data from users:

Data you provide us directly

  • Email address – for account creation and communication
  • Username – selected by the user
  • Profile picture – optional, user-submitted image

Data we collect automatically

  • Device information – model, OS version, hardware (non-identifiable)
  • Geography/region – for localization (non-precise location)
  • Operating system and app version – for compatibility purposes
  • Application usage data – app opens, first launches, and updates
  • In-app purchases – to manage subscriptions or paid features
  • Cookies and similar technologies – for login sessions and usage tracking

Some of this data is linked to your user identity (e.g., email address, purchases). Other data is collected anonymously or is not linked to your identity.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To create and manage your account
  • To personalize your experience within the app
  • To provide access to premium features
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and onboarding
  • To respond to user support requests

WHO CAN SEE YOUR DATA

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing. Data may be processed by service providers (e.g., analytics or subscription tools) solely to support app functionality.

USER CONTROLS AND RIGHTS

  • Users can delete their account at any time directly within the app under the account settings menu.
  • You can update your profile information or email address from within the app at any time.
  • You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at nebitrams@gmail.com.
  • App permissions (e.g., screen time access) can be revoked in your device’s Settings under Screen Time or Privacy.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy as our app evolves. You will be notified of any major changes via email or in-app notification.

CONTACT

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at nebitrams@gmail.com.

Thank you for using Reclaimr!
